# Acceptable Use — Repeat Violators Policy

This document summarizes our enforcement approach for prohibited monetization categories to support payment compliance requirements.

1) Graduated Enforcement
- First violation: content removal and formal warning; educational guidance provided.
- Second violation: temporary monetization suspension (7–30 days) and acknowledgment requirement.
- Third violation: permanent monetization ban and potential account termination.
- Severe violations may trigger immediate escalation (e.g., permanent ban or termination).

2) Reviews, Holds, and Compliance
- We may pause or reverse payouts, disable charges, or restrict features during investigations.
- We may share necessary information with the payment processor for risk, fraud, or compliance review.

3) Appeals and Recordkeeping
- Users may appeal within 14 days of an action; provide context or evidence to expedite review.
- We maintain audit logs of violations and actions taken.
